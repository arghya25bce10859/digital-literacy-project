DO'S:

- Think before posting things online and how they may affect your future career as a professional.
- Stay Professional and make sure your bio and posts stay respectful and proper.
- Check the credibility of your sources and make sure the information they give you is trustworthy before sharing it.
- Connect with and learn from industry professionals and focus on engaging in meaningful interactions.
- Check and update your privacy settings frequently to make sure your data is safe.



DON'T:

- Overshare your personal details and sensitive data with others online.
- Impulsively post or respond to people online in an emotional state.
- Infringe Copyright laws by not crediting creators for their work and achievements.
- Refrain from sharing academic material with your peers when you should.
- Engage in Cyberbullying by using digital platforms to harass the people around you in any way.
